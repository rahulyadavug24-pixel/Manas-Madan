About BharatBreed

BharatBreed allows easy, AI-powered identification of Indian cattle and cow breeds from your photos. Our advanced technology helps farmers, veterinarians, and cattle enthusiasts quickly identify breeds with high accuracy. 🐄✨

Uploaded images are processed by our AI to identify the breed using advanced computer vision trained on thousands of cattle photos. The AI analyzes distinctive features to match with known breed characteristics.
Our AI model analyzes features like color, shape, body structure, horn shape, ear size, and markings to extract distinguishing details about the breed. It examines over 100 different characteristics for accurate identification.
Accuracy depends on photo quality; our AI is trained for high reliability (over 95% accuracy with clear images), but clear, focused images yield the best results. Well-lit side profile photos work best.
For best accuracy, upload photos with a single, clear cattle. The result may be less accurate if there are multiple animals. Our system will try to focus on the most prominent animal in the image.
Ensure good lighting, full body side view, and a clear view of distinguishing features when capturing your photo for upload. Avoid blurry images and make sure the animal is facing sideways for best results.
